About the Movement

Discover our global mission to transform the skies into a universal canvas for peace and unity.

The Main Event: Kite Air Show

Fly for Peace is culminating in an extraordinary Kite Air Show Event on April 22nd, 2026, at Bentota Beach, Sri Lanka. We invite the world to join this worldwide movement that uses the universally celebrated sky as a canvas for unity. We believe that cultural expression and international collaboration possess the power to transcend borders.

Our Mission

To unite individuals worldwide through peaceful expression, fostering an environment where nations collaborate in harmony.

Our Vision

A world where the sky is only filled with colors of freedom, kites of joy, and symbols of human connection—never conflict.

Fly for Peace

A global initiative promoting unity, harmony, and peaceful coexistence. Air is not for war; Air is to breathe.
